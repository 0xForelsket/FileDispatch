---

## Using Templates

1. Click **Templates** in the Rules panel header
2. Browse or search for a template
3. Click a template to preview
4. Configure variables (like destination folder)
5. Click **Install Template**

---

## Template Variables

Templates may include configurable variables:

| Variable Type | Example |
|--------------|---------|
| Path | Destination folder |
| Text | Custom prefix or suffix |

Variables are filled in before installation and become part of the rule.
